# Shiva Sahasranama (Linga Puranoktam, Vishnu kritam)

Chanting Bhagavan Nama is considered an important way to rid our sins, difficulties and sufferings and obtain health and prosperity both of material and spiritual kind, especially in the Kali Yuga. Sahasranama-s have a special role in such nama japa. They pave the way for unhurriedly meditating on the many qualities of Bhagavan and also doing laksha archana, koti archana etc.

There are many Sahasranama-s of Bhagavan Shiva. Of these, the Linga Purana Purva Bhaga 98th Adhyaya contains the Sahasranama which Bhagavan Vishnu used to worship Him by a thousand lotuses.

Our Shri Kanchi Kamakoti Mulamnaya Sarvajna Peetadhipati Shri Chandrashekharendra Sarasvati Shankaracharya Swamigal has blessed that by the chanting of this Sahasranama signifying Hari-Hara unity, the disunity seen in various levels of society will be solved for people to lead a happy life and work towards higher goals.

This Sahasranama Stotram with Namavali is released in multiple scripts for parayana/archana on Maha Shivaratri, other Shiva puja-s, or as per the wishes of devotees.
