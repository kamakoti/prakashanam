# Mrityunjaya Manasa Puja Stotram

The practice is to pray to Bhagavan that we should live without misery and move to the next world without difficulty, and to grant us bhakti which is the sadhana for this.

Since mrityu means the suffering of samsara and Bhagavan blesses to win over it, He becomes Mrityunjaya. Since it is important that the mind is involved in puja, manasa puja becomes important.

Shri Shankara Bhagavatpada's Mrityunjaya Manasa Puja Stotram which shows this path is published in multiple scripts with summary translation.
