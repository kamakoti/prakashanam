## Veda Dharma Shastra Paripalana Sabha (established CE 1942)

# Bhishma Tarpanam

Bhishma Pitamaha whose was Dharma Svarupi made a vow that he would observe brahmacharya vrata throughout His life and lived as a brahmachari only. Hence He did not have any descendants. However all of us attain shreyas by the very many dharma-s like Vishnu Sahasranama taught by Him in the Mahabharata Shanti Parva and Anushasana Parva-s.

Therefore, on Magha Shukla Ashtami all of us should perform tarpanam and arghya danam unto Bhishma. (This includes those whose father is alive.) It bestows endless punya and destroys all papa-s accumulated during the last year.

On this auspicious day, let us all do anushthanam accordingly in the path shown by our Kamakoti Acharyas, and obtain the anugraha of Bhishma Pitamaha! The procedure for this is published in multiple scripts.
