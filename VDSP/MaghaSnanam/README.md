# Magha Sn─Бnam

In our Sanatana Dharma, sn─Бnam is capable of removing all our papam and giving prosperity. Sn─Бnam during the month of Magha is significant according to many Puranas. 

Hence, in this month, Maghasn─Бnam should be done every day in the prescribed manner alongwith Sankalpa. If that is not possible, it should be performed at the start of the month, Rathasaptami, Masi Amavasya. In case, that too is not possible, anushthanam of this procedure must be done at least on one day. 

Those who cannot do sn─Бnam in places such as a tirtham may chant the same mantras and do the sn─Бnam at home.
