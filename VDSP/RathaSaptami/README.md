# Ratha Saptami

Surya Bhagavan visibly shows us that there is a Divine Power beyond humans. As He obtained his chariot (ratha) on Magha Shukla Saptami, it is called тАЬRatha SaptamiтАЭ. He is very benevolent on that day. Snanam, danam etc on that day please Him, remove all poverty and yields innumerable benefits. Thus says Skanda Puranam. 

On this auspicious day, let us all do anushthanam accordingly in the path shown by our Kamakoti Acharyas, and obtain the anugraha of Surya Bhagavan! The procedure for this is published in multiple scripts.
