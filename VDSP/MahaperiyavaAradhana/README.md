## Aradhana of Shri Chandrashekharendra Sarasvati Shricharana

Margashirsha Krishna Dvadashi is the Aradhana of Shri Chandrashekharendra Sarasvati Shankaracharya Swamigal, 68th Acharya of our Shri Kanchi Kamakoti Moolamnaya Sarvajna Peetam. 

The Mahaswami said "Only because Shri Adi Shankara Bhagavatpada incarnated and preserved Dharma, we are able to observe all our festivals today. So we should worship Him."

Likewise, we are all indebted to Shri Chandrashekharendra Sarasvati Shricharana, Shri Jayendra Sarasvati Shricharana and Shri Shankara Vijayendra Sarasvati Shricharana who benevolently continue to teach us our Sanatana Dharma Sampradaya. 

So let us worship our great Guru Parampara on this occasion and receive Their anugraha!
